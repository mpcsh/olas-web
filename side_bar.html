Experiencia e Identidad

This year, OLAS's annual art fair theme is Latinx identity. The theme aims to recognize and celebrate the cohesiveness and power that come from shared experiences and unity in diversity, both in Latin America and abroad. The fair will take place at the end of this Week! See you there!

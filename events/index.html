Our Events

OLAS is committed to planning and executing a wide variety of events that cater to our community. In order to do this, we have two types of events: Series and Individual.

Series events are long-running events that center around the same theme (e.g. Habla Con OLAS focuses on spreading a basic understanding of the Spanish Language).

Individual Events are events whose topics and themes are highly manuable, often reflect important current events, and tend to be submitted by our membership rather than just the board.
